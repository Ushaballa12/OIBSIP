Kim Taehyung

also known as V

Biography

Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, South Korea, V is known for his deep, soulful voice, charismatic stage presence, and unique sense of style. V's exceptional vocal abilities and expressive performances have earned him widespread acclaim from both fans and music critics. His versatility as a vocalist allows him to tackle a wide range of genres, from smooth R&B to powerful rock ballads. With his captivating stage presence and infectious energy, V has a natural ability to command the audience's attention. Whether he's performing on stage or engaging with fans during interviews, his charming personality shines through. V's bold and eclectic sense of style has made him a fashion icon in the K-pop industry. Known for his daring fashion choices and trend-setting looks, he effortlessly blends streetwear with high fashion, inspiring fans around the world.
